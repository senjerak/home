Tech Artist | Student

Experiencing Art is the Greatest Act of Empathy.

There's just something magical about stepping into someone else's mind; seeing what they feel is truly beautiful. I enjoy art even when I don't like it. Unconditionally loving all of the things that make me happy in life is exactly why I create.

Firstly, I have to thank my Grandmother: Hortencia Lecusay.

Without her, I would not be where I am today. I feel the warmth of her appreciation in the Florida sun. It was the love of my family, my friends, and the people closest to me that gave me the confidence to believe in myself and my work.

I express my loyalty, devotion, and passion within my artwork. Every project is another opportunity to improve upon the last. My dedication is brought out by the details of my restless efforts. I care about my loved ones and I care about our planet. My goal as an Artist is to inspire other trans youth like myself to allow themselves to be happy. When the world is against you, it is easy to forget that you are worth it.

Thank you for making it this far :^)
